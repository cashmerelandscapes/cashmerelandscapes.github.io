Jungle fencing

Back breaking jungle clearing

They dont make it easy in Nelson. This job had 45% grade bank that was covered with bush lawyer, blackberrys and the worse ivy and old mans beard. I cleared out 100 cubic metres of jungle and then put a fence around the property to enclose a goat who would take over the jungle encroachment duties! Hard work, steep country but the view was excellent and the results speak for themselves.

Cashmere Landscapes is a Christchurch based Landscape Design/Construction and Garden Maintenance business owned and operated by David Sutherland.

David has over 25 years experience in the industry and has worked for other Landscaping businesses before setting up Cashmere Landscapes.

David is passionate about the environment and can talk to you about any residential or commercial project, whether it be starting with a blank site, or enhancing an existing site. He has an extensive knowledge of plants and good supply contacts within the industry.

David has been a Regional Councillor for ECAN and involved with Forest & Bird, and in the past with the Avon Heathcote Ihutai Trust. He is a Board of Trustee member at Cashmere Primary School and an active member of the wider Cashmere community.
